What is this?

Slideborg is designed for presenters and has a couple use cases:

  • Say you're at an unconference and you're short a projector, but most everyone has their own laptop or tablet. Share a Slideborg url with your audience, and they can view your slide deck while you control it from your own device.

  • This time you're at a conference that has a projector, but you don't want to stand at your laptop to control it. A remote would sure be handy, but you don't have one :( Just load up a Slideborg url on your laptop, and control it via your phone or tablet.

There are other possibilities as well! Imagine putting on a remote presentation where no one is in the same location. We hope you enjoy!

What slide decks are supported?

Currently we support deck.js, impress.js, and reveal.js.

Support for SlideShare and Speaker Deck is in progress.
